We Design Tomorrow

Monolith is a global architectural consultancy built on a singular premise — that great architecture doesn't just exist in the world, it elevates it.

Architecture should speak of its time and place, but yearn for timelessness.
— Arthur Pendelton, Principal Architect

A Studio Built on Vision

Founded in 2012 by Arthur Pendelton in New York City, Monolith was born from a shared frustration with architecture that prioritised novelty over meaning. We set out to build a studio where structural integrity, human experience, and aesthetic boldness were not competing values, but one unified philosophy.

Twelve years later, we operate from offices in New York, London, and Singapore, with a portfolio spanning over 150 projects across 30 countries. Yet our commitment to craft, collaboration, and client partnership remains unchanged.

Our Values

🔬

Research-Led Design

Every project begins with deep research — into the site, the culture, the climate, and the client. We don't design in a vacuum; we design in context.

🤝

Client Partnership

We treat every client as a true collaborator. Your vision drives everything — we simply provide the expertise, tools, and artistry to realise it.

🌱

Sustainable by Default

Sustainability is not a checkbox for us — it is embedded in every decision, from material selection to orientation, energy modelling to landscape integration.

⚡

Innovation First

We embrace technology as a tool for human expression, using parametric design, BIM, and VR to push the boundaries of what's architecturally possible.

🏆

Pursuit of Excellence

Good enough is never good enough at Monolith. We pursue excellence in every detail, from the grand skyline silhouette to the grain of a timber handrail.

🌍

Global Perspective

With studios on three continents, we bring a genuinely global perspective to every project — culturally informed, internationally standardised.
